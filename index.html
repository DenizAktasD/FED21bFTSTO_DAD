WHO WE ARE

We are the ones with the funny name. DAD stands for Deniz Aktas Design, and contains the initials of the owners name.
It is also a tribute to the owner's dad! The moustache is his :)

Our passion is designing and printing business cards for both small and large businesses.

Our goal is to create something that you proudly can give away and that will make the receiver remember you.

Also, the love for paper can be seen in the great variety of weights and quality,
and the aim to find the ultimate combination of feeling and look for your card.

WHAT WE OFFER

We will provide you with what you need, want and can afford.

You can use your own design and just let us do the printing or get help all the way from idea to a finished product.

We can deliver in any way needed and the production time is fast.

It shouldn't be hard to get a good design, and we guarantee that you will be satisfied!


If you have your own design we can offer you three kinds of packages:

– The small offer that contains one set of 50 cards printed in one colour.
– The medium offer that contains ten different sets of cards, printed in full colour.
– Last, but not least, the large package containing 25 different sets od cards in full colour delivered in a separate box for each set.
